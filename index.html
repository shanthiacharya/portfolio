About Me

I am a Senior UX & Interaction Designer at GE Design & Experience Studio in San Ramon California. Prior to that I worked at Western Digital, Kno Inc. and IBM. Before moving to a UX career I worked at Intel as a SW Engineer.



I started my career as a software engineer in Intel. Over the course of time, I developed a passion for UX and pursued human factors degree. I have since gained experience in interviewing stakeholders, quantitative and qualitative research, usability testing, interaction design, wireframing and agile methodologies.



When I am not working I do kitchen table math with my kids, experiment with recipes, learn classical Indian music, or practice zumba/bollywood dance at the gym.
